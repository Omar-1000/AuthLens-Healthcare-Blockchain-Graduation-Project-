AuthLens Healthcare Blockchain

Protecting Sensitive Healthcare Data from Cyber Attacks

AuthLens is a secure e-health platform that gives patients control over access to their medical records. It combines encrypted off-chain storage, blockchain integrity proof, and identity-aware approval workflows.

1 Problem
  • !Unauthorized access to sensitive patient records.
  • IDWeak identity verification in digital healthcare systems.
  • HMedical data can be modified without clear tamper proof.
  • VPatients have limited visibility over who views their data.
2 Solution
  • OKPatient-approved access before a doctor can view records.
  • BCBlockchain hashes anchor record integrity and approvals.
  • LEncrypted medical records remain off-chain for privacy.
  • WWallet authentication and audit logs improve accountability.
